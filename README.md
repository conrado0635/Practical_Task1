## Privacy Checklist 
### Principle 1: Purpose of collection of personal information.
* Consider 1:
     * How are we going to explain the activities that needs the collection of personal information and why such collection of information is needed for such activities.
* Actionable Item 1:
     * The  activities and functions must be provided and explained well so that the individual or user will understand the purpose of collection of their information. There must me an easy-to-understand explanation.     
 * Consider 2:
     * How are we going to specifically provide for the purpose of collection of personal information that will be understood by users.

 * Actionable Item 2:
     * Users may be confused or refuse to provide personal information if they cannot understand the specific purpose. It must well readable and easy-to-understand and often-used terms.
 ### Principle 2 - Source of Personal Information 

 * Consider 1:
     * How are we going to avail consent to collect personal information from the user?

  * Actionable Item 1:
     * Users usually provide consent before giving their personal information. There must be a part that asks and collects the consent of the individual that his/her personal information  will be collected.
 * Consider 2:
     * How are we going to ensure that the personal information is from the individual concerned?
 * Actionable Item 2:
     * Users have the ability to provide personal information or other's personal information or fake personal information.
